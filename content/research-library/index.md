# PBM Research Library

Welcome to our comprehensive research library on photobiomodulation (PBM) and related therapies. This collection includes peer-reviewed studies, clinical trials, and systematic reviews.

## Research Categories

### Brain & Cognitive Function
Studies on PBM effects on brain health, cognitive performance, and neurological conditions.

**Featured Papers:**
- Transcranial photobiomodulation for brain disorders
- Effects on cognitive function in healthy adults
- Neuroprotection in traumatic brain injury
- PBM for neurodegenerative diseases

### Mental Health
Research on PBM applications for anxiety, depression, and mood disorders.

**Featured Papers:**
- Light therapy for seasonal affective disorder
- PBM effects on anxiety and stress
- Applications in depression treatment

### Athletic Performance
Studies on PBM for muscle recovery, performance enhancement, and injury prevention.

**Featured Papers:**
- Muscle recovery and performance optimization
- Reduction of exercise-induced fatigue
- Injury prevention and rehabilitation

### Clinical Applications
Research on specific medical conditions and treatment protocols.

**Featured Papers:**
- Chronic pain management
- Wound healing and tissue repair
- Inflammation reduction
- Sleep quality improvement
